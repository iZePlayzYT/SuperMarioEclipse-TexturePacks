Super Mario Eclipse – 4K Texture Pack (DDS)

This texture pack was created for Super Mario Eclipse, a ROM hack of Super Mario Sunshine.
It improves the visuals with high-resolution textures optimized for Dolphin Emulator.
👉 The texture pack is also FULLY COMPATIBLE with original (vanilla) Super Mario Sunshine.

━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
INSTALLATION
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

1. Install the latest version of Dolphin Emulator:
   https://dolphin-emu.org/

2. Enable custom textures:
   - In Dolphin, go to Graphics Settings → Advanced
   - Enable: Load Custom Textures
   - Disable: Prefetch Custom Textures

3. From this ZIP, copy the included "GMSE04" folder into your Load/Textures directory:
   Windows: C:\Users\<YourUsername>\AppData\Roaming\Dolphin Emulator\Load\Textures\
   macOS:   ~/Library/Application Support/Dolphin/Load/Textures/
   Linux:   ~/.local/share/dolphin-emu/Load/Textures/
